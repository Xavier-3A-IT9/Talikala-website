About Talikala
In order to assist prostituted women and give them the tools they need to rescue themselves from their "enslavement," Talikala was created. Talikala was founded in August 1987 by individuals and prostitution survivors. Talikala dedicates itself to the process of women's empowerment and to improving the public's perspective of prostituted women because it recognizes prostitution as a phenomenon that sustains class and gender inequities in our society today. Talikala also pledges to help prostituted women exercise their rights, reclaim their dignity, and define their place in society as well as to build channels for public education in an effort to alter public perceptions of prostituted women. Therefore, it seeks to enhance the lives of women who are sex trafficking and prostitution survivors
