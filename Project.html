VISIONARIES FOR WOMEN!










Background & Problem

What prompted our group to create this project?


Even with its ongoing success, Talikala Inc. faces direct issues in sustaining activities for girls and their parents within the communities, influenced by financial inadequacy to cover the costs of the activities they provide. The organization requires a substantial amount of funding to fully support its beneficiaries: to provide food and transportation are two instances. When the number of beneficiaries rises, so does the need for more manpower and resources, amplifying Talikala's lack of publicity which restrains the institution from gaining access to more opportunities through public exposure. This prompted our group to initiate a problem response which is Digital Footprints: Expanding Talikala’s Online Presence.

Significance

What is the importance of our project towards the community?


Given that we are in the age of information, the significance of electronics and technology has paved the way for societal growth. While Talikala faces the challenge of lack of exposure, which can be said to be interlinked with their financial inadequacy, our group aims to take advantage of the media and internet by expanding the digital footprints of Talikala with our online website — through this, we are able to cast more light on the institution and attract potential benefactors that could assist in meeting the unmet needs of the institution and attain a diverse audience empowered to support the institution's mission. In accordance with this, our project also avails improved awareness amongst not only Ateneo students and personages, but for the general public to engage with.









Pre Implementation

The pre-implementation of our proposal will start with the proposal writing, feasibility study, then audience analysis, and output plan finalization from October 12, 2022 to November 14, 2022.

Implementation

For the actual implementation, it will start on Nov 15, 2022 to Feb 28, 2023 with the activities: website developing, commercial Video developing, visual Media Developing, testing and debugging, and the establishment & launching.

Post Implementation

From March 1, 2023 - onwards, during post-implementation of our project, the team will do feedbacking, website enhancements and maintenance.
